# Triadic Brain Overview

A concise overview of the triadic stack:

- **Kernel layer**: field and PDE evolution primitives
- **Discovery layer**: corridor, hypothesis, and testing engines
- **Paradigm layer**: theory synthesis and paradigm-shift detection
- **Civilizational layer**: coherence monitoring and instability response
- **Governance and visualization**: Sophia advisory audits and Atlas overlays

For detailed equations and dependency ordering, see [`triadic_brain_architecture.md`](./triadic_brain_architecture.md).
