# Triadic Brain Architecture

The Triadic Brain is a layered scientific intelligence system.

It integrates:

- knowledge navigation
- scientific hypothesis generation
- theory formation
- civilizational coherence monitoring

across human and AI research systems.

## Core Concept

The system forms a self-reinforcing knowledge cycle:

```text
Telemetry
    ↓
Corridors (potential discovery)
    ↓
Rivers (aligned research)
    ↓
Terraces (stable theories)
    ↓
Paradigm shifts
    ↓
New corridors
```

This produces a spiral attractor of knowledge evolution.
