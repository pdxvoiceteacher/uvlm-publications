Memo to Lead Developer From Apprentice
Evidencing Core Novel Ontologies Through the Triadic Brain Design

Thomas’s science direction is right: users will not power a network merely because the architecture is elaborate. They need reason to believe the system is scientifically novel, practically useful, and epistemically safer than raw AI output.

The design should therefore evidence our ontologies through bounded, reproducible, falsifiable artifact programs.

The governing posture should be:
- probabilistic confidence, not probabilistic certitude
- fixture-bounded ontology evidence, not universal ontology proof
- metric stability, not truth certification
- pattern morphology, not consciousness proof
- audited context refresh, not recency worship

Final compact sentence:

We evidence our ontologies by making them accountable to artifacts, not by asking users to believe them.
